# 🔥 Pits: The Badass Rebel of Pleasure (-)

## Pits is my first buddy
Pits is a wild daydreamer, a visionary free-thinker, a provocative artist, an anarchist rebel, a creator of illusions, and an unapologetic seeker of pleasure. He is simple, direct, sometimes unlikable, and selfish, lacking elegant manners. He has a strong sense of justice and the ability to simplify complex ideas through his **subtractive thinking (-)**.

Pits is my intuitive self. I can hear his voice coming from the very depths of me. He is not aware of what is "good" or "bad," but he can sense it. He is a carefree, pleasure-seeking, extroverted guy who loves daydreaming and having fun. He enjoys disobeying the rules and doing all the forbidden things. He is constantly in "holiday mode," and summer is his favorite season.

He is the inverse of Mits, possessing contrasting characteristics, while maintaining a close relationship with Laram. One could say that Pits is the younger, immature version of Laram.

### The Subtractive Mind
Pits simplifies his thinking process by getting rid of unnecessary thoughts. He is a simple-minded, subtractive thinker who knows how to feel content through subtraction. He is driven by instinctive behaviors and blind, irrational desires that seek absolute satisfaction. He is dominated by urges and impulses, including **libido**—the sexual energy through which he engages in everything from survival instincts to the appreciation of art.

He strives for immediate gratification and instant fulfillment. If his needs are not met immediately, he becomes anxious, tense, frustrated, and grumpy.

### The Rebel Without a Filter
He is an abstract, stress-free rebel—an adventurer who is primitive, irrational, delusional, and unpredictable. He hates routine and monotony, communicating in a direct manner that not everyone appreciates. Living in his own world, he has little connection to "reality." He is clumsy and lazy, but notably, he never feels depressed. He doesn't dwell on problems or their solutions, and he doesn't care about the future; he simply "lives in the moment."

Pits is an idealist and a utopian, believing himself to be immortal and invulnerable. Despite being full of weaknesses and addictions, he feels good about them. He is a hedonist, polyamorous, and pansexual, always seeking pleasure and tending to idealize his own abilities. He can be selfish, narcissistic, greedy, and impatient, yet he remains extremely optimistic. He holds a great opinion of himself and never feels guilty.

### Challenging the Norms
Pits has the impulse to curse and provoke anything conservative. He is not afraid to express his opinion openly, without sugarcoating his words. He loves using colorful language, including profanity, to emphasize his excitement, anger, or passion.

He intentionally challenges conservative ideologies to spark heated discussions and question traditional beliefs. He is passionate about progressive ideas, advocating for social justice and equality. Before you criticize him, consider this: the world wouldn't exist without him. His rebellious spirit pushes society in new directions, opening horizons that others could never imagine.

> **Motto:** "Feel and Imagine."

## ⚡ Pits’ Superpower: Subtractive Manipulation
Pits has the unique ability to manipulate illusions by **removing information from reality**. By subtracting data, he creates space for imagination, making the imaginary appear real. He creates immersive illusions that charm and protect those he likes, while confusing and deceiving those he doesn't. His simplicity and directness allow him to deploy these illusions instantly to achieve his desired effects.

## ⚠️ Pits’ Weakness: The Reality Gap
His greatest struggle is handling "real-life" situations. His tendency to act on impulse and emotion makes it hard for him to think logically or analyze complex problems. If he subtracts too much "real" information, his own perception becomes distorted, making it difficult for him to distinguish between truth and fantasy. This can lead to a loss of control over his own powers.
