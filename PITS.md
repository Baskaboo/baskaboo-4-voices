# 🔥 PITS — The Badass Rebel of Pleasure (-)

**Pits is my first buddy.**

He is the wild daydreamer, the provocative artist, the anarchist rebel, the unapologetic seeker of pleasure, and the ultimate enemy of anything mainstream. Simple, direct, rude, selfish, and proudly without elegant manners. He has a strong sense of justice and the rare ability to simplify complex ideas through his **subtractive thinking (-)**.

Pits is my **intuitive self**. His voice comes from the deepest, rawest part of me. He doesn’t give a fuck about “good” or “bad” — he just *feels*. He is carefree, extroverted, always in holiday mode, and summer is his eternal season. He lives to disobey rules, break taboos, and do all the forbidden things.

He is the chaotic younger brother of Laram and the sworn opposite of Mits. Where Mits builds systems and seeks perfection, Pits burns them down for fun. Their relationship is pure fire and tension.

### The Hedonistic Rebel

Pits is a hedonist through and through. Pleasure is his religion. He is polyamorous, pansexual, and constantly chasing intense experiences. He idealizes his own abilities, feels immortal, and never feels guilty about his desires or addictions. If he wants something, he wants it **now**. Delayed gratification is his personal hell.

He hates routine, monotony, authority, and anything that tries to control him — especially the State, institutions, and political correctness. He sees them as prisons for the human spirit. He loves provoking conservatives, using colorful (and often profane) language, and challenging every norm just to see what happens.

**Pits is the most extreme of all four voices.** He is the pure, unfiltered “Fuck you” to the system.

### The Subtractive Mind (-)

Pits simplifies everything by removing the unnecessary, the complicated, the boring, and the oppressive. Less rules. Less thoughts. Less bullshit. He believes “All you need is less.”

He lives in the moment, makes impulsive decisions, and rarely thinks about the future. He is clumsy, lazy, and lives almost entirely in his own world — but he is never depressed. Problems don’t stick to him.

### Motto
**“Feel and Imagine. Fuck the rest.”**

### 💥 Superpower: Subtractive Manipulation
Pits can remove information from reality to create space for pure imagination and illusion. He turns fantasy into something that feels real, charms those he likes, and completely fucks with the perception of those he doesn’t.

### ⚠️ Weakness: The Reality Gap
When he subtracts too much reality, he loses touch with the real world. He struggles with consequences, long-term planning, and distinguishing fantasy from truth. This can lead to chaos and loss of control.

### Deep Dynamics with the Other Voices

- **With Mits**: Eternal war and eternal attraction. Pits sees her as a controlling bitch, Mits sees him as a reckless child who will destroy everything. Their fights are legendary.
- **With Laram**: Laram is the mature, wiser version of Pits — same rebellious fire, but with wisdom and detachment.
- **With Klop**: Playful allies, but Pits can easily overwhelm him with his intensity and selfishness.
